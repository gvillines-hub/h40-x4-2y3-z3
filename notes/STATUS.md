# Status ledger — three-tier discipline

Last updated: June 12, 2026.

## Tier 1 — Proved (unconditional)

1. The reduction: every primitive solution of x⁴ + 2y³ + z³ = 0 corresponds to a
   rational point on one of the six listed genus-2 curves. (Elementary algebraic
   number theory in ℤ[∛2]; PARI-verified identities.)
2. A−(ℚ) = B+(ℚ) = C−(ℚ) = ∅ — fake 2-Selmer sets empty, Magma TwoCoverDescent with
   default unconditional class-group proof level. (Runs 2, 7, 8.)
3. A+(ℚ) = {∞±, (1, ±3)} — rank 1 < genus, classical Chabauty, N = 5. (Run 4.)
4. B− and C+ each have trivial torsion and Mordell–Weil rank exactly 2, with exhibited
   generators; they are quadratic twists by −2 and are non-isomorphic over ℚ.
   (Runs 1, 5, 6.)
5. Fake 2-Selmer sets of B− and C+ are singletons; all their rational points funnel
   through the cover D: w² = Q(x) over K = ℚ(∛2), where C₃ = γ·Q with N(γ) = C₃.
   (Runs 7, 8; PARI identity checks.)
6. E = Jac(D) has j = 1728, rank E(K) = 3 exactly, and its −2-twist has rank E'(K) = 0;
   consequently rank E(K(√−2)) = 3. (Runs 9, 13.)
7. At p = 43: every point of E(K) with ℚ-rational image under the cover map u lies in
   one of 8 explicit cosets of the subgroup generated by the known points — exactly the
   classes of x ∈ {1, −1, 2, 6/5} and their hyperelliptic partners. (Run 14.)
   Caveat: this statement is relative to the finite-index subgroup generated by the
   exhibited points; saturation/index-coprimality checks are routine and pending.

## Tier 2 — Numerically certified (not yet formally proved)

1. End⁰(Jac(B−)⊗ℚ̄) = M₂(ℚ(√−2)): integral endomorphism basis of size 8 computed by
   Sage RiemannSurface at 128 → 400 bits, residuals ≤ 10⁻¹²⁰; center minimal polynomial
   x² + 2. Corroborated by exact Euler factors at 26 primes (trace vanishing at all
   inert primes; π²-factorization at all 16 tested split primes ≤ 200).
2. The endomorphism field contains the degree-24 compositum of the identified degree-8
   and degree-6 entry fields; conjecturally equals the degree-48 maximum.
3. Six numerical involutions of B−; 24 numerical twist maps B− → A+-model; none defined
   over fields of degree ≤ 6.
4. The conductor of B−/C+ has odd part 3⁶ (PARI genus2red, rigorous); the 2-part of
   Magma's value 186624 for A+ printed under Ogg-formula warnings and is not relied on.

## Tier 3 — Conjectural

1. **Main conjecture:** the only primitive integer solutions of x⁴ + 2y³ + z³ = 0 are
   (±1, 0, −1), (±1, −1, 1), (±43, −203, 237). Equivalently: the only rational points
   on B− are (1, ±1), (−1, ±15) and on C+ are (2, ±12), (6/5, ±172/125); equivalently,
   the rational x-values on the cover D are exactly {1, −1, 2, 6/5}.
2. Identified rigorous closing routes: (a) quadratic Chabauty over F = ℚ(√−2)
   (rank J(F) = 4, two independent idele class characters, ρ(J/F) ≥ 2 via the
   GL₂(F)-type structure — the Balakrishnan–Besser–Bianchi–Müller regime); (b) refined
   descent combining the conic factor γ with the quartic cover.
